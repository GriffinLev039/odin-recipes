Blackened Salmon

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp ground paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 tbsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano
  • 4 salmon filets, skinned and deboned
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Directions

  1. Mix paprika, cayenne pepper, onion powder, salt, white and black pepper, thyme, basil, and oregano together in a small bowl.
  2. Brush salmon filets on both sides with 1/4 cup butter, and sprinkle evenly with the spice mix.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp melted butter in a large skillet over high heat. Add salmon and cook till blackened (2-5 minutes). Lift fillets, add remaining butter to skillet, and flip fillets into butter. Cook until the other side is blackened and fish flakes easily with a fork (3-5 minutes).
  4. Serve and enjoy!
